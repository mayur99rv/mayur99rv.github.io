after nearly two months of infinite scrolling on insta, I finally decided to retrospect my actions.

the realization that I have been wasting my time was depressing !

the dawn - a new beginning

but i guess, trying to get out of depression isn't good enough motivation to do something awesome.

i needed bigger regrets to become productive.thats why, i wasted another month doing absolutely nothing

yet another dawn - A life changing revelation

days were getting normalised as if depression was the new normal for me. i'll admit one thing here, social medias are addicting, i was getting my dosages of dopamine and serotonin from it.

the hormones which were usually secreted upon achieving a sense of accomplishment, were abundantly available to me for free! the reason for my laziness , the untold truth, was lying in front of me,naked.

That fear at base,is a very common fear in beings. But if we will dare to look past it, we will find something worthy of ourselves.
